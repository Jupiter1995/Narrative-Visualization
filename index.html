Background

On March 11, 2020, the World Health Organization (WHO) declared COVID-19, the disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2, a pandemic. The announcement followed a rising sense of alarm in the preceding months over a new, potentially lethal virus that was swiftly spreading around the world.

Two year later, we look back on one of the most challenging periods in recent memory. During this challenging periods, many countries have to made hard decision between health and economic. Here, we will look back and focus on the fection rate solely among five most powerful countries in the world, which are the permanent members in The Security Conuncil of UN - US, UK, France, Russia and China.
